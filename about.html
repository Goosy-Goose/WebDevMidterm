Mango Valley Games

Mango Valley Games is an independent game company based in Irvine, California (right next to Blizzard lol i'm not even joking) with a second location in New York City.

With an affinity for horror, we aim to put out games that leave you with a story to remember.

We specialize in RPGs, but we have been known to dabble a bit into shooters. In other words, there's nothing we won't attempt.


Our Story

MVG was founded by a student who wanted to tell a story. Since then, we've honed our abilities and continue to improve our games. We are firm believers that games give audiences immersive storytelling experiences that have the power to change lives.

We want to create works of art that resonate with people around the world. Each game we make is lovingly crafted from the tiniest detail to the biggest picture in order to give players unique experiences with every game they play.
